Grey

Dark is used for body copy and Title 1 and Title 2 text. Base can be used for copy that's secondary to titles (subheadings). Light TBD.

Blue

For elements/icons that are expanding on other content, as a descriptor. Use for size indicators for design chellenges (small/medium/large).

Green

Use for action-based (links and link icons, primary buttons) elements.

Red

Used very sparingly, to guide attention of user.
